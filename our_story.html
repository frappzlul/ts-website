First Established in 2017, when some friends were messing around.

Team Scarlet's roots come from when a group of three school friends were messing around, and our original name was concepted, that being "The Super Gamers", which was shortened to TSG, and the meaning was changed in 2018 to "Tactical Scarlet Gaming", However, in early 2019, we went on hiatus, until in March of 2020 when we returned under the new name, Team Scarlet.

The Second Team Scarlet (March 2020 - December 2020)

The Second Team Scarlet comprised of a peak of 29 members, ranging between FORTNITE and VALORANT. In total, three teamtages were made, and we grew the channel from 12 Subscribers to 150. Our VALORANT division was disbanded in September, however the Fortnite Division was put on hiatus after six of our primary Fortnite Players were released. The New Team Scarlet - Our Projection
‍
We plan to relaunch Team Scarlet after our second hiatus on December 18th 2021, with four new divisions, and a new goal for our impact in eSports and gaming. We plan to create a space where gamers can learn, play and overall have a good experience. We plan to allow any and all into Team Scarlet, under our (soon to form) Project SYNTAX. We also intend to create a beacon for the gamers of the Caribbean, for it is often thought that the Caribbean does not have an impact on the esports industry nor the gaming industry. Join us, on our road to a new beacon for esports, and for the hope and inspiration of gamers across the region.

December 18th, 2021. See you there.
